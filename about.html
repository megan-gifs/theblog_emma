About (my magnets)

These magnets consist of a variety of shapes, textures, materials, size, etc. Some were gifts, some bought on vacation, while others were found in a free box on the side of the street. Each one has its own story, and today I will tell my three favorite stories, and hopefully you learn a little about me through my favorite magnets.
